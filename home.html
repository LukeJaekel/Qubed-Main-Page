ABOUT

Qubed Audio was founded by Joe Hughes with the intention of providing quality sound at an affordable rate. Joe has been working in both the live and studio side of audio since the age of 16. Over the last few years we have had the pleasure to have worked with many big companies and venues in both the Bournemouth music scene and further afield with clients such as Absolute Music, BBC Radio Solent, Slick Events, SJHire, ThamesTV, ModestTV, LEDHouse & Catch The Fire as well as many others to name a few supporting the audio side on various small, medium and large scale productions. Whether we provide the engineering services or the kit to carry out the job, the idea behind Qubed Audio is to provide specialist knowledge, great sound and equipment from the world of audio in the most professional and most cost effective package possible.
